Hi, I'm Andrew Rametta

I am a full-stack web developer, living in Tampa, FL. My developer background includes building full stack applications from the ground up, updating code to add features and fix bugs, and creating useful tools that benefit the users. I spent the first part of my career in the nonprofit world. My passion for work has always come from the drive to help people.

I am looking for a great team to join. I enjoy working with others and believe that two heads are always better than one. I think having a diverse group of people with vastly different perspectives makes for the most creative teams.I bring a unique perspective through my experiences in the nonprofit world. My favorite thing about software development is knowing I will never know everything. I am a lifelong learner, and it excites me to know that learning is a part of the job.

A little about me...

From the first time I printed "Hello World" in my console I was hooked! I became a software developer because I wanted a career that required me to use my brain to help solve problems that make people's lives better. I have always sought out opportunities and challenges that are meaningful to me. My professional path has taken many twists and turns to get to where I am today. My work as a pastor at a church gave me the skill of listening to others and wearing many hats. Working at Lululemon for five years helped me grow attention to detail while folding yoga pants and leading a visual merch team. My most recent experience as a fundraiser for nonprofits has taught me what motivates people. Throughout this crazy journey, I've never stopped engaging my passion for helping others and solving problems along the way.

When I’m not coding, I am usually sweating in a small hot darkroom, doing yoga. I like to open up my fridge and pretending I am competing on an episode of Chopped. The motto I live my life by is Hakuna Matata.

My current focus is in Accessibility, React, and Design Systems.

My Projects

PayItForward

GitHub Live Link

PayItForward is a donation platform that connects donors to local non-profits. Just sign in, post a picture of what you want to donate, and PayItForward will connect you with the non-profits in your area that can take your donation. A non-profit will private message you to set up the details for receiving your donation. PayitForward makes it simple and easy for you to find a good home for the things that you want to get rid of.

Built with: React(Hooks), Node.js, Express, PostgreSQL

Daily Gratitude

GitHub Repo Live Link

"Something as simple as writing down three things your are grateful for every day for 21 days in a row significantly increases your level of optimism, and it holds for the next six months." - Shawn Anchor Daily Gratitude is a gratitude journal web application where you can log three daily gratitude entries. Our hope is that as you make this a part of your daily routine, you will start to feel more optimism toward your life as you focus on and remind yourself of those things that you are grateful for.

Built with: React, Node.js, Express, PostgreSQL

Quarantine Date Night

GitHub Repo Live Link

Quarantine Date Night is designed for anyone looking to plan a perfect quarantined date night in! All you need is to input the food cuisine you are in the mood for and any dietary restrictions you might have and let us find the perfect recipe. Next, input the genre of movie you are want to watch and you will be given 20 popular movie titles of that genre to explore with a plot description to help you decide. We hope that with some inspiration from the search results you can pick the perfect recipe and movie for your next quarantined date night in.

Built with: HTML, CSS, JavaScript, jQuery, and external APIs
